# Structured Completion Report

Use this exact structure for the final Patch 10 completion report.

## SUMMARY
- Brief summary of the refinement pass outcome.

## FILES / PAGES AFFECTED
- List touched pages, shell files, shared components, and governing docs.

## AUDIT FINDINGS
- Report audit findings grouped as BLOCKING, IMPORTANT, or ENHANCEMENT.

## ISSUES FIXED
- List resolved issues from the refinement pass.

## BLOCKING ITEMS REMAINING
- List unresolved blocking items.

## IMPORTANT ITEMS REMAINING
- List unresolved important items.

## PRICING ITEMS REQUIRING FOUNDER APPROVAL
- List every still-unapproved public price or package-boundary decision.

## CHECKS PERFORMED
- Record regression searches, reviews, builds, tests, and manual validation.

## BILINGUAL / LOCALIZATION RISKS
- Record any EN/ES parity risk or pending human editorial review.

## PRIVACY / DISCLOSURE RISKS
- Record any unresolved workflow, policy, confidentiality, or claim risk.

## ACCESSIBILITY RISKS
- Record any unresolved semantic, keyboard, contrast, focus, or mobile-usability risk.

## PROOF / CONFIDENTIALITY RISKS
- Record any unresolved proof overexposure or client-identification risk.

## REQUIRED HUMAN DECISIONS
- List decisions that cannot be guessed and still require approval.

## RELEASE RECOMMENDATION: HOLD / PROCEED
- State one recommendation only.
