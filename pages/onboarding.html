Client access

Client onboarding

Client onboarding is not delivered through the public site. It is provisioned only after a written engagement is in place and a secure workflow has been assigned.

Release rule

No onboarding data should be entered into this public build.

—
Why
This repository does not contain a protected client intake route, secure file handling, or release-approved disclosure for onboarding data.
—
When access opens
Access opens only after kickoff preparation is complete and the client team receives a workflow-specific secure channel.
—
What to avoid
Do not send regulated, confidential, billing, or client operational information through the public site until a secure route is explicitly provided.

If you are an active client awaiting onboarding access, contact your project lead directly through the secure channel provided at engagement kickoff.
