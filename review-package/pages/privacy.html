Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide through our website forms: name, email, organization, and project details. We do not collect information automatically beyond standard server logs and analytics.

How We Use Information

Information submitted through Start an Engagement is used to assess project fit and prepare proposals. Information submitted through Careers is used to evaluate applicants. Information submitted through Inquiries is used to respond to your correspondence.

Data Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Data Retention

Project inquiry data is retained for 12 months. Applicant data is retained for 24 months. General inquiry data is retained for 12 months. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Data Security

Data is stored securely with encryption in transit. Access is limited to authorized team members only.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. Contact us through the Inquiries page to exercise these rights.

Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact us through the Inquiries page.
